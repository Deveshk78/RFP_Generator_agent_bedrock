REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL: Enterprise Cloud Migration Services

Issuing Organization: Metro Health Systems
RFP Number: MHS-2026-CLOUD-001
Submission Deadline: August 15, 2026 at 5:00 PM EST

1. BACKGROUND
Metro Health Systems operates 12 regional clinics and requires migration of
on-premises workloads to AWS, including EHR integrations, imaging systems,
and internal business applications.

2. SCOPE OF WORK
- Assess current infrastructure and produce migration roadmap
- Migrate 80+ VMs and 15 TB of data to AWS
- Implement HIPAA-compliant architecture
- Provide 90-day post-migration support

3. MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS
- Vendor must hold AWS Advanced Consulting Partner status
- Minimum 5 years healthcare cloud migration experience
- Provide references from at least 3 healthcare clients
- Submit signed NDA with proposal

4. EVALUATION CRITERIA
- Technical approach (35%)
- Healthcare experience (25%)
- Cost (20%)
- Timeline (10%)
- References (10%)

5. SUBMISSION FORMAT
Submit PDF proposal (max 40 pages) via email to procurement@metrohealth.example.org
